Integration steps

First step

Application for connection

Apply for connection by filling out the registration form to get customized conditions for your business. Wait until the compliance of your project is finalized (3 to 7 working days). If necessary, you will be contacted to clarify details and receive some documents from your company.

Second step

Opening an account

After the project is approved, all data for access to your personal account will be sent to your e-mail specified during registration. The mail will also receive notifications about each stage of integration. Once you have access, contact your connection manager to open accounts and agree on service tariffs.

Third step

Testing

After opening accounts, you can start setting up terminals for Internet acquiring through our service. Follow the instructions described in our technical documentation and use the test environment to check the set parameters. Once all settings are completed, you can accept the first payments from customers!
